Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner in
Fabrication Engineering

Since 2019, M/s Designo has been bringing innovative trolley manufacturing and fabrication engineering solutions to life. Located in the Saraswati Industrial Complex, Makardah, Howrah, we serve businesses seeking precision and quality of any kind of trolley.

A manual trolley is a fundamental material-handling device designed to transport heavy or bulky loads across short distances without the use of an external power source. It relies entirely on human effort — pushing or pulling — to move.

Depending on the industry, our manual trolleys span several different designs, each engineered to handle the specific demands of your operation.
